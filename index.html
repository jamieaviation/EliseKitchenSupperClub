In memory & tribute to my dear Mother whom was an amazing cook; who specialised in recipes of South East Asia, Sri-Lanka and India to produce fantastic culinary delights .

The aim of this supper club is to serve fantastic dishes inspired by my Mother's cooking. Ranging from the sumptious dishes such as Satay and chilli crab of Malaysia; to the simple hoppers served with curry of Sri-Lanka, amazing Thai inspired pad thai noodles and the Vietnamese Pho soup noodle.

The supper club is held twice a month and will cater for up to 20 diners. We normally serve 3 courses, with the main course comprised of up to 6 delicious dishes spanning the best of SE Asian cuisine.

(Note: Sorry, the menus are not suitable for vegetarians as it is not easy to substitute the ingredients in the dishes. We do accommodate non-meat eaters with seafood and fish dishes with prior notice.)

The supper club is held at residential addresses in NW London.

Seating is on communal dining tables where you can meet other food lovers and, hopefully, make some new friends. We aim to make the evening fun and convivial.

We are happy to cater for groups, couples and singles. We will do our best for you to have a thoroughly enjoyable evening.
